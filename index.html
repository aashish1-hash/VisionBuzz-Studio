05 — Case study

Nirvana Skincare: from 4K to 90K followers.

A full rebuild of content strategy and creative system over 5 months.

Be

Before

Inconsistent posting, no brand voice, ad spend with no creative refresh — growth had completely flatlined at 4,000 followers.

St

Strategy

Rebuilt the content pillars around founder story, education and UGC. Introduced a 3-post-a-day cadence with a repeatable creative system.

Ex

Execution

Shipped 140+ pieces of content, ran always-on Reels experiments, and paired every top performer with a paid amplification budget.

Re

Results

In five months, the account went from stagnant to the fastest-growing in its category.
